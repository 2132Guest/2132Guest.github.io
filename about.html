Suhaib Ahmad: A True Car Enthusiast

Suhaib Ahmad's love for cars is more than just a hobby; it's a deep passion that drives his everyday life. From a young age, Suhaib was drawn to high-performance vehicles, fascinated by the precision and design of luxury brands.

German Engineering Passion

BMW M5: The perfect balance of luxury and performance, with a 4.4L V8 producing 600+ horsepower.

Mercedes-AMG GT: Handcrafted 4.0L V8 biturbo engine with signature AMG exhaust roar.

He equally admires Mercedes-Benz for its luxury and cutting-edge technology. The GLS 600 represents the pinnacle of full-size luxury SUVs, while the G63 AMG combines brutal off-road capability with lavish interiors.

Italian Thrills

Suhaib is captivated by the thrill of driving sports cars, especially Ferraris. The SF90 Stradale (shown above) blends hybrid technology with 986hp of raw power, while the 812 Superfast delivers a naturally aspirated 6.5L V12 masterpiece.
