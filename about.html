Suhaib Ahmad: A True Car Enthusiast

Suhaib Ahmad's love for cars is more than just a hobby; it's a deep passion that drives his everyday life. From a young age, Suhaib was drawn to high-performance vehicles, fascinated by the precision and design of luxury brands.

German Engineering Passion

Among his favorites are BMWs, known for their perfect blend of power and elegance. The BMW M5 holds a special place in his heart with its combination of luxury sedan practicality and supercar performance.

He equally admires Mercedes-Benz for its luxury and cutting-edge technology. The iconic G-Wagon represents to him the pinnacle of rugged luxury, capable of both city cruising and off-road adventures.

Italian Thrills

Suhaib is captivated by the thrill of driving sports cars, especially Ferraris. He admires the raw power and engineering brilliance behind models like the 812 Superfast, considering them not just cars but experiences.

"There's nothing like the scream of a Ferrari V12 at full throttle," Suhaib often says. "It's mechanical poetry in motion."

The Dream Collection

His dream garage would include:

  • BMW M5 CS (for daily driving perfection)
  • Mercedes-AMG G63 (for luxury off-roading)
  • Ferrari 812 Superfast (for weekend thrills)
  • Porsche 911 GT3 RS (for track days)

"Cars are rolling art to me - each model tells a story of engineering passion and human achievement."
- Suhaib Ahmad
